Videogames for a New Anthropological Image

v1.22.0-alpha.1
A thesis submitted in fulfilment of the requirements for the degree of
Doctor of Philosophy


Ben Evans

Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

Faculty of Arts and Education
Deakin University
August 2023

This project performs an anthropology of video games and a videogame of anthropology, presented in the form of an interactive website. The videogame is not merely an image to analyse, but a complex human event to be participated in. The project seeks to reveal its transcendental status, as a condition for new styles of thought and experience. It also responds to the inherent violence of representation, in a way that extends beyond videogames and to the human images anthropology generates.

Proceeding entails your participation in the production of violent images.
